About The District

The Ga East Municipality is noted for its well-planned physical layout. which has attracted many Real Estate Companies such as Oak Villa, Regimanuel Gray, Edlorm Estate etc to the municipality. It also has some private residential areas in places like Agbogba, Haatso, Dome, Taifa, Kawbenya and some other places The municipality comprises of some commercial buildings too which are used for commercial purposes such as banks, consulting firms and office complexes. There are some properties which are also used for health facilities.
